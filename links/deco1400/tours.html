Tours

Come take a look at our tours!

As this resort has just opened, we're currently only offering one tour. Do expect more exciting tours and attractions in the near future!

The Casual Tour
Day 1

Note: For people who are unable to ride on the butterflies, our esteemed tour guides will prepare tram rides for you.

12PM: Check in and Shrink down

Prepare to shrink yourself into a tiny size so that you're able to stay in our resort!

1PM to 2PM: Resort Tour and Lunch

Find out more about the beauty of our shrunken yet exquisite resort, and experience the majesty of the resort.

2PM to 2.15PM: Butterfly Feeding

Get acquainted with the butterflies by feeding them nectar and water!

2.15PM to 2.30PM: Butterfly Ride

Experience one of our main attractions--flying on a butterfly! Ride to our Sunflowers, where we have a zip-line of activities waiting for you.

2.30PM to 4PM: Sunflower Ziplining

Learn more about these giant stalks, and unleash your inner child by ziplining your way through them!

4PM to 4.30PM: Butterfly Ride

We will be riding our way towards the Basils.

4.30PM to 5.30PM: Basil Exploration

We have the option of either exploring the basil plants or relax on the leaves and enjoy its aroma. You have the option of staying with our tour guides as they will be trekking along the basils and talking about them.

5.30PM to 6PM: Butterfly Ride

We will be riding back to the resort.

6PM to 6.30PM: Rest

Take a break in your own hotel room.

6.30PM to 7.30PM: Dinner in the Resort

We serve high quality food with fresh ingredients! *Note: Please indicate your dietary requirements if you decide to book a tour.

7.30PM onwards: Freeroam

You're free to partake in our resort facilities!

Day 2
9AM to 10AM: Breakfast

Indulge in our healthy and tasty buffet!

10AM to 11AM: Rest and relaxation

Partake in our pools or facilities in the resort!

11AM to 11.15AM: Butterfly Ride

We will be riding to the Butterfly Harvest.

11.15AM to 12PM: Butterfly Harvest

We will be witnessing the magnificent harvests of the butterflies as they collect pollen from our top quality flowers.

12PM to 1PM: Harvest Lunch

Partake on our Butterfly Cafe near the harvest for lunch!

1PM to 2PM: The Bridge of Beauty

Walk along our Bridge of Beauty--the bridge where you can touch our flowers and crops, and look at them up close in a huge size! Our tour guides will talk about the flowers in the eyes of our butterflies and insect life.

2PM to 3.15M: Butterfly Ride

We will be riding back to the resort.

3.15PM to 4.15PM: Freeroam

You're free to rest in your room and/or partake in our resort facilities!

4.15PM: Check out and Grow Back

We hope you have a pleasant stay! Feel free to partake on the non-restricted areas of the greenhouse as your regular size.
