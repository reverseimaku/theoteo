Our Shrink Machine has been approved by Queensland Government to operate, and adheres strictly to Australian Safety rules. There are no side effects in visitors who have used our Shrink Machine.

All the certified butterflies are friendly and 100% safe to ride. Safety gear is provided and is required to be equipped before riding a butterfly. However, if you or someone you know are/is squeamish about riding a butterfly, you can opt for our Monarch or Morpho butterflies, as our Peacock butterfly rides can be intense. However, persons who are unable to ride the butterflies due to physical disabilities, or pregnant people, can opt to go for our trams if they are interested in the attractions.
