Attractions

Sunflower Zip-lining

Do you know that the brown centers of a sunflowers are actually individual flowers? Come and look close up at what they look like, and learn more about these iconic flowers with our special tours! Then face your fears by ziplining down them!

Basil Roaming

You’ve used basil as a herb. You’ve seen basil as small plants that pepper up your food. You’ve smelled basil in small quantities. But have you ever experienced basil in a worm’s eye view? Relax and sunbathe on the leaves of the basil plant, or spend some time hiking with these giant plants, giving you an adventurous enchanting experience! The choice is yours!

Butterfly Harvest

This is one of the most vibrant attractions we have! The butterfly harvest consists of our butterflies harvesting flowers and collecting pollen from them. On top of that, you also get to enjoy the sight with our very own shrunken Butterfly Cafe!

Bridge of Beauty

Ever want to see just how smooth an eggplant is? Or how the tiny flowers in a sunflower actually look like? Now you can get a chance to see the (technically) giant flowers and crops up close! Get a chance to observe these diverse and vibrant crops in a whole new light as you walk through the bridge, and even touch them!
